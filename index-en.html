Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use?

I work mainly with alpaca, bronze, and copper. These noble metals allow for durable designs with unique character.

Do you ship?

Yes, I ship nationwide. Delivery time and cost depend on your location. Feel free to contact me for details.

Can I request a custom piece?

Absolutely. If you have an idea or want to adapt a design to your style, let’s create it together.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Keep it in a dry place, avoid contact with perfumes or creams, and clean it with a soft cloth. This helps preserve its shine and shape.

Where can I see your work?

You can explore the catalog on this site or follow me on Instagram for updates, events, and new releases.
